# Basic Info

[[Cities and Towns/Jkaverstelle/Kithraia/Roseström University\|Roseström University]] is the national university in [[Nations/Jkaverstelle\|Jkaverstelle]] located in its capital, [[Cities and Towns/Jkaverstelle/Kithraia/Kithraia\|Kithraia]]. In its halls, learned professors teach various subjects to the aristocratic elites and sponsored talents lucky enough to get a ticket for admission. The school is currently headed by Chancellor [[Roogie Rosewood\|Roogie Rosewood]], a middle-aged [[Smallfolk\|Smallfolk]] woman and member of the House [[Rosewood\|Rosewood]].

Currently, [[Cities and Towns/Jkaverstelle/Kithraia/Roseström University\|Roseström University]] acts as one of the few remaining points of diplomatic contact between [[Nations/Jkaverstelle\|Jkaverstelle]] and [[Nations/Anbuur\|Anbuur]]. Specifically, scholars of [[Cities and Towns/Jkaverstelle/Kithraia/Roseström University\|Roseström University]] and [[Khazzüld\|Khazzüld]]—the [[Nations/Anbuur\|Anbuuri]] national university, and an ancient academic stronghold—have enjoyed relatively cordial collaboration. Approved academics are granted special permissions to pass between these nations, though even that has grown rarer in recent years.   
# History

[[Cities and Towns/Jkaverstelle/Kithraia/Roseström University\|Roseström University]] was originally chartered 145 years ago (3568 ASC) as a physical declaration of the newly formed alliance between House [[Rosewood\|Rosewood]] and the [[Forsström\|Forsström]] royal family, meant to serve as a place of higher learning for those under the banner of "[[Nations/Jkaverstelle\|Jkaverstelle]]".
# People

## [[Roogie Rosewood\|Roogie Rosewood]]

[[Roogie Rosewood\|Roogie Rosewood]] is a middle-aged [[Smallfolk\|Smallfolk]] woman of House [[Rosewood\|Rosewood]] and is the current chancellor of [[Cities and Towns/Jkaverstelle/Kithraia/Roseström University\|Roseström University]].
## [[Tronny Ego\|Tronny Ego]]

[[Tronny Ego\|Tronny Ego]] is an older [[Smallfolk\|Smallfolk]] man and is the current Head Curator of [[Cities and Towns/Jkaverstelle/Kithraia/Roseström University\|Roseström University's]] Rare Books and Materials Library. Before taking his current position, he was an acclaimed professor of [[The Ages/Second Age\|Second Age]] studies.
